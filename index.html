Welcome to Janmashtami 2023
By Indian Institute of Technology Mandi
Get ready to immerse yourself in the vibrant hues of culture and spirituality as the prestigious IIT Mandi gears up to host a grand Janmashtami festival on September 7th. With a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, the event promises to be a captivating celebration of Lord Krishna's birth. The campus will be adorned with intricate decorations, and a joyous atmosphere will fill the air as students, faculty, and visitors come together to commemorate this auspicious occasion. From soul-stirring bhajans and mesmerizing performances depicting Krishna's leelas to thought-provoking talks by esteemed scholars, the festival will offer a holistic experience that transcends boundaries. The IIT Mandi Janmashtami festival is set to be a spectacle of devotion, unity, and cultural splendor, fostering a sense of togetherness and leaving an indelible mark on all who partake in its revelries.

About IIT Mandi
Nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes of the Himalayan foothills, IIT Mandi stands as a beacon of academic excellence and innovation. Renowned for its emphasis on holistic education, cutting-edge research, and a close-knit community, IIT Mandi offers a unique learning environment that nurtures both intellect and character. The institute encourages students to explore diverse fields and ideas, transcending conventional boundaries.

The campus's serene ambiance and state-of-the-art facilities provide an ideal backdrop for students to thrive and engage in meaningful academic pursuits. As a trailblazer in engineering and scientific education, IIT Mandi continues to shape the future by producing individuals who are not only skilled professionals but also socially responsible global citizens.
